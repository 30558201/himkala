About Himkala

Himkala is a premium eCommerce platform dedicated to offering authentic food products, exquisite embroidery pieces, and natural beauty products (such as shampoos, soaps, and personal care items).

Our Natural Promise

We take pride in offering products that are completely homemade and prepared using traditional, natural methods.

  • We only use the essential ingredients required for the product.
  • No unnecessary chemicals, preservatives, or acidity regulators are added.
  • We always strive to make our products safe, natural, and beautifully prepared while maintaining the highest standards of quality and hygiene.

Our ultimate goal is to provide pure, natural, and high-quality homemade products prepared with genuine care.

Delivery & Support

Free Delivery: We are proud to provide free delivery across India for all our customers.

Customer Contact: We stand by our products even after they arrive. Customers can always contact us for support after delivery if they have any questions, concerns, or need assistance.

Return Policy

Due to the nature of our natural and homemade goods, products generally cannot be returned once delivered.

However, returns may be accepted strictly under valid situations, such as:

  • The product was damaged during delivery.
  • The issue is thoroughly reviewed and verified by our support team.

Returns will be processed only after proper review and approval by Himkala.

Important Notice:
If you are unsure about the product or do not wish to keep it, please avoid placing the order to help us prevent waste.
Privacy & Customer Data

Your trust is important to us. Customer information—such as your name, address, and contact details—is collected and used strictly for order processing, delivery, and providing customer support.

We guarantee that your personal data is secure with us and will never be misused, sold, or shared with unauthorized third parties.
